Commission Details Overview

Welcome to the Commission Details page. Here, you'll find everything you need to know about how commissions are earned, the different types of commissions available, and a detailed breakdown of your earnings.

Commission Structures

Referral Commission

Earn commissions by referring new users to the platform. For each user that signs up and makes a deposit, you earn a percentage of their deposit amount.

Bet Commission

Earn commissions on the bets placed by the users you referred. The more they bet, the more you earn!

Bonus Commission

Special bonuses or promotional commissions are available during certain events or milestones. Keep an eye out for these opportunities!

Commission Tiers

As your activity level increases, you may qualify for higher commission tiers, earning you even more for your efforts.
