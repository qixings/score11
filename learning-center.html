Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I place a bet?

    To place a bet, select the game you want to play, choose your bet amount, and confirm your bet by pressing the "Place Bet" button.

  • How do I know if I won?

    Your game will notify you with a win message if your bet is successful, and your account balance will be updated automatically.

  • What are the different types of bets?

    There are various betting types, such as single bets, multi-bets, and special game bets. You can find more details in the game rules section for each game.

  • Can I play for free?

    Yes, many games offer a "practice mode" where you can play without using real money. This is great for learning the mechanics before you start betting.

  • How do I withdraw my winnings?

    You can withdraw your winnings by navigating to the "Withdraw" section, choosing your payment method, and following the on-screen instructions.
