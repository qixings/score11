VIP Levels & Rebate Ratio Overview

Unlock exclusive benefits as you progress through VIP levels by meeting specific betting and referral criteria. Each VIP level offers better commission rates, monthly rebates, and additional perks. Learn how to advance and maximize your rewards below.

VIP Level Progression Criteria

To advance through VIP levels, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Betting Requirement: The total amount you have wagered.
  • Subordinates Required: The number of direct referrals you've introduced to the platform.

Monthly Rebate Explanation

Each VIP level comes with a fixed monthly rebate, which is paid out based on your VIP level at the end of each month. The rebate amount is determined by the combination of your betting activity and the number of direct subordinates.

Note: The higher your VIP level, the greater your monthly rebate, offering more rewards for your dedication and success.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are VIP Points calculated?

VIP Points are calculated based on your total betting amount and the number of direct subordinates you introduce.

What is the monthly rebate, and how is it applied?

The monthly rebate is a fixed amount that you receive at the end of each month, based on your VIP level. It is credited to your account automatically.

What happens if I don’t meet the betting requirement?

If you don’t meet the betting requirement for a VIP level, you remain at your current level until you reach the necessary criteria to advance.

How often are VIP levels updated?

VIP levels are reviewed and updated at the end of each month based on your activity during that period.
