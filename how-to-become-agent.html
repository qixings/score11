Eligibility Criteria

To qualify as an agent, you must:

  • Be registered and have a complete account in the platform.
  • Be 18 years or older.
  • Reside in an eligible region.
  • Recruit 5 active users who make deposits and remain active for at least 30 days.

How to Become an Agent

No application is required! Simply follow these steps:

  • Focus on recruiting 5 active users who make deposits through your referral link.
  • Once you've successfully recruited 5 active users, you'll automatically be notified of your agent status.

After becoming an agent, you will:

  • Gain access to exclusive agent tools and resources.
  • Start earning commissions on the activities of your recruited users.
  • Receive bonuses for reaching higher recruitment milestones.

Commission Structure

As an agent, you can earn:

  • Direct Commissions: Earn 10% on the first deposit made by each user you recruit.
  • Team Performance Bonuses: Earn additional commissions based on your team's collective activity.
  • Milestone Bonuses: Unlock bonuses by reaching recruitment milestones (e.g., 10, 50, 100 active users).

VIP Levels & Commission Structure

As an agent, your VIP level will determine your commission rate and the benefits you receive. The more subordinates you have, the higher your VIP level and rewards.

Responsibilities of an Agent

  • Promote the platform ethically and avoid any misleading tactics.
  • Support your recruits and ensure they remain active on the platform.
  • Comply with all terms and conditions outlined by the platform.
  • Maintain the confidentiality of any sensitive information you access as an agent.

Support and Resources

As an agent, you'll have access to:

  • Marketing resources, such as banners and referral links.
  • Priority support from our agent assistance team.
  • Exclusive bonuses as you progress through the VIP levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my progress toward becoming an agent?

You can view your recruitment progress in your user dashboard, where you'll see how many active users you've recruited.

What qualifies as an active user?

An active user is someone who registers through your referral, makes a deposit, and remains active on the platform for at least 30 days.

When will I be notified of my agent status?

Once you've successfully recruited 5 active users who meet the criteria, you'll automatically receive a notification confirming your agent status.
